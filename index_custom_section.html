Our Process

1. SUBMIT YOUR IDEA

Send us your sketches, moodboards, or a design brief.
Preferred file formats: AI, PDF, OBJ, STL, PNG
No files? No worries – our design team can bring your concept to life from scratch.

2. MATERIAL & FILE PREPARATION

Our experts can adapt your file or craft a custom 3D model tailored to your vision.
Custom 3D design services start at €100 for accessories and €200 for garments.
3D components are printed using recycled PLA.

3. SAMPLING & TEST SELECTION

We bring your idea into reality with a printed sample on your selected material.
Sampling starts at €60 for accessories and €150 for garments, depending on complexity and scale.

4. APPROVAL & PRODUCTION

Once you're happy with the sample, we begin production.
Choose between limited runs (1–5 units) or full collection production.

5. FINISHING & DELIVERY

We finish your piece to perfection – whether it's a cut-out detail, full panel, or complete garment.

About Us
